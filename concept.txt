Positioning

1.Position:static;
Ans:- By default the position is static.so no changes seen by normal eyes.

2.Position:relative;
top,bottom,left,right.
Ans:- you can simply move the image(box) relative to its Original position.


3.Position:Absolute;
top,bottom,left,right.
Ans:-you can simply move the image(box) relative to the nearest parent element.

4.Position:fixed;
bottom:0px;  ,top:0px;
Ans:-Img(box) getting fixed in particular position.If you scroll still it is not moving.

5.Position:Sticky;
Ans:- inside scrolling website there will be scrolling content. See example online.
